E-Commerce Platform Feature Overview

Client: Oasis Water Date: March 11, 2026 Status: Production-Ready
✓ Fully Functional

🚀 Platform Overview

Enterprise-grade e-commerce platform for B2C and B2B water delivery operations. Features intelligent order management, bulk ordering optimization, flexible fulfillment options, and comprehensive customer experience tools designed to maximize operational efficiency and revenue.

🛒 Smart Cart Management

$50 minimum order with real-time validation, checkout blocking, and clear user alerts. Bulk ordering system with MOQ rules, increment-based quantities, and intelligent error messages suggesting valid alternatives (e.g., "Try 8, 12, or 16 cases").

🚚 Flexible Fulfillment

Delivery & Collection modes with multiple saved addresses, default selection, time slot scheduling (3 options), date picker, and collection point mapping. Optimizes route planning and customer convenience.

👤 Customer Experience

Profile management with saved addresses, order history, contact preferences, and secure data handling. Address management includes "Default" badges and in-checkout addition for streamlined repeat purchases.

💳 Payment & Trust

Multiple payment options: Cash on Delivery, EcoCash Mobile Money, Card payments. Customer testimonials, business showcases, service guarantees, and quality assurance build credibility.

📊 Order Management

Admin dashboard with real-time tracking, status filtering, order details view, collection schedules, and comprehensive management interface. Reduces errors and support queries.

🏢 B2B & B2C Platform

Dual-market support with category filtering, separate pricing models, smart recommendations, featured products, and professional imagery. Serves both individual and business customers seamlessly.

📈 Business Value Delivered

Revenue Growth Higher average order value through bulk ordering incentives and $50 minimum cart requirement.
Operational Efficiency Optimized delivery routes, time slot scheduling, and reduced order errors.
Customer Retention Streamlined repeat purchases with saved profiles, addresses, and payment methods.
Market Expansion Single platform serves both B2C and B2B markets with tailored experiences.
Reduced Support Costs Real-time order tracking, smart validation, and clear messaging minimize queries.
Payment Flexibility Cash, EcoCash, and card options reduce cart abandonment and maximize conversion.
